Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

1. Data Collection

"Golos" extension does not collect, store, or transmit your voice recordings or text data to our servers.

All voice processing is handled locally or via your browser's Web Speech API.

2. Permissions

  • Microphone: Used solely for speech recognition during active sessions.
  • ActiveTab / Scripting: Required to insert recognized text into the active page field.
  • Offscreen: Used for audio feedback notifications.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

1. Data Collection

We value your privacy. "Golos" is designed to operate as autonomously as possible.

We do not record, store, or transmit your voice or dictated text to our servers. All processing happens locally within your browser using standard Web APIs.

2. Why do we need permissions?

Chrome may ask for specific permissions during installation. Here is why we need them:

  • Microphone:
    Activated only when you press the Start button or the hotkey. It is used solely to convert your speech to text in real-time.
  • Page Access (ActiveTab):
    This permission is required only to insert text automatically into the field where your cursor is placed. We do not read your browsing history or analyze website content.
  • Audio (Offscreen):
    Used to play short sound notifications (ON/OFF), so you know exactly when the recording starts and stops.

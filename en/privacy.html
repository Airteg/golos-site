Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

1. Key Facts About Your Data

We respect your privacy. The "Golos" extension is designed to work as autonomously as possible.

We do not record, store, or send your voice or dictated text to our servers.

Speech recognition is performed using Google Chrome's built-in engine (Speech Recognition). Depending on browser settings, capabilities, and language, Chrome may process audio either locally or via its online services. Golos does not control this process and does not have its own recognition servers in this version.

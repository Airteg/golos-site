#### 1. Why this extension exists

**Golos** is a Chrome browser extension that allows you to dictate text by voice and automatically insert it into any editable text field: email, messengers, documents, forms, chats, and other websites.

The extension is designed for:

- fast text input without a keyboard;
- saving time;
- comfortable work with large amounts of text;
- correct punctuation during dictation.
